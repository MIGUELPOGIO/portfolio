## 🎯 Pro Tips

1. **Update Regularly** - Don't wait until submission. Update CV and portfolio as you complete projects.

2. **Quality Over Quantity** - 3-5 well-documented projects look better than 10 incomplete ones.

3. **Show Your Process** - Include commits showing your development process, not just final results.

4. **Be Specific** - Instead of "Built a website," say "Built a React e-commerce platform with payment integration using Stripe."

5. **Quantify Results** - "Reduced load time by 40%" is more impressive than "Optimized performance."

6. **Keep It Fresh** - Add new projects throughout the term, update skills as you learn them.

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Should I include every project?**
A: No. Include 3-5 of your best projects that showcase different skills.

**Q: Do I need a personal website?**
A: Not required, but it shows initiative. GitHub can serve as your portfolio.

**Q: How often should I update?**
A: Add projects as you complete them. Update CV with new skills/certifications quarterly.

**Q: What if my projects aren't polished?**
A: Use this as motivation to clean them up! Add documentation, fix bugs, remove outdated code.
